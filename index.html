Published scientist, now retrained as a Data Scientist and Software Engineer.

I specialize in R programming, which I use both to build web apps (Shiny), APIs (plumber), packages, websites and interactive dashboards (Quarto), as well as to analyze a variety of data types.

I was involved in several research projects at the intersection between Neuroscience and Biomedical Engineering. I have worked with biology/medical data, but also behavioral, surveys, energy, geography/spatial, and sales/marketing data.

I am very fond of statistical modeling (especially of the Bayesian sort), and have an (unhealthy) obsession with the R ecosystem and its community.
